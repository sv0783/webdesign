Recipe

Blueberry Muffins

Directions

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees, grease 12 cup muffin tin
  2. Stir flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in large bowl, make well in center
  3. Beat egg,whisk milk and oil in small bowl.
  4. Pour egg mixture at once into flour mixture;mix quickly and lightly until moistened. Batter will be lumpy
  5. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full
  6. Bake in preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes.
